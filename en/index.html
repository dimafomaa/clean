Frequently asked questions

When should general cleaning be done?

General cleaning must be carried out regularly, at least twice a year (recommended in spring and autumn). Most often, it is worth carrying out general cleaning in apartments with small children or if there are pets in the house. In such cases, it is necessary to carry out gen. cleaning at least once a month. If your apartment has not had a general cleaning in the last 3 months, it is time to order it from EcoClean.

What works does the general cleaning service include?

When performing general cleaning, contamination is removed on all horizontal and vertical surfaces from floor to ceiling, in hard-to-reach places - behind furniture, radiator grills, inside lockers. Stains are removed: from fat, dried liquids, lime scale on tiles and sanitary ware, rust, scale. Disinfection of surfaces is carried out.

Is window cleaning included in cleaning?

Window washing is not included in the basic general cleaning package, but it can be ordered additionally.

Are quality guarantees provided?

Yes, we guarantee that cleaning services will be performed with high quality. If within 24 hours from the moment of assembly you find minor defects, employees will come and remove them for free!

How long will cleaning/dry cleaning last?

The duration of cleaning is primarily influenced by the type of cleaning, as well as the area of ​​the room and the level of contamination. Several main factors that affect the duration of cleaning: the number of rooms, mortgaged premises, the area of ​​the apartment, house, office. Daily cleaning takes 2 to 4 hours, general cleaning takes 3 to 7 hours, and cleaning after a fire can take several days. Dry cleaning also depends on the degree of contamination and takes approximately 30 minutes per seat, dry cleaning of a carpet at home takes about an hour per 10 square meters.

How effectively are stains removed?

If the stain can be removed at all, then we will remove it. As a rule, it is possible to completely remove 95% of stains, or make them almost invisible. However, stains associated with a violation of the fiber structure, the breakdown of dyes, especially complex pigment contamination cannot be completely removed in principle without destroying the material. Removing stains and cleaning carpets is a job that requires high qualifications. The main principle is not to make it worse.

Is it safe to stay indoors while dry cleaning?

Yes, all the chemistry we work with is professional and hypoallergenic, certified and safe for both humans and animals. Active components with pungent odors are sometimes used for severe contamination and complex stains. However, after dry cleaning of carpets, furniture or mattresses, no chemicals remain in the products.
